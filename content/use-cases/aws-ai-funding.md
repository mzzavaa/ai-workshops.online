## AWS Funds AI Prototypes

One of the most underutilized resources for organizations exploring AI is the AWS funding program ecosystem. AWS funds proof-of-concept (PoC) projects through several mechanisms - covering up to 100% of AWS service costs for qualifying projects.

This means your first AI prototype can cost you nothing in cloud infrastructure.

## Which Programs Are Available

**AWS PoC Credits**
Short-term credit programs designed to support initial experimentation. Typically cover compute, storage, and AI/ML service costs during a defined PoC period. Eligibility depends on company size, AWS relationship, and project scope.

**AWS Marketplace Partner Programs**
If you work with an AWS partner (like an AI consulting firm) to deliver the project, additional funding tiers become available. The partner acts as the delivery vehicle; AWS covers the infrastructure costs.

**AWS Generative AI Accelerator**
Specific programs exist for generative AI projects, particularly those using Amazon Bedrock. These are newer programs with active funding pools.

**Industry-Specific Programs**
Certain industries (media, public sector, healthcare) have dedicated funding tracks with specialized support teams.

## Eligibility Considerations

Not every project qualifies, but the bar is lower than most organizations assume. Key factors:

- The project must use AWS services (not just run on AWS)
- There must be a clear business case and defined success metrics
- The organization should be willing to share a case study if the project succeeds
- Timeline: most PoC programs are 4-12 weeks

## How the Process Works

1. **Scope the project** - Define the use case, expected AWS services, and success criteria
2. **Assess eligibility** - Work with an AWS partner or account team to identify the right program
3. **Submit the application** - The partner typically submits on your behalf
4. **Receive credits or funding commitment** - Approval timelines vary but are often 2-4 weeks
5. **Deliver the PoC** - Execute within the agreed scope and timeline
