## The Challenge with AI Projects

Most AI initiatives stall before they start. Organizations spend months in strategy sessions, vendor evaluations, and pilot planning - and then the initiative loses momentum, budget, or executive sponsorship before any code is written.

The 3-workshop method exists to break this pattern.

## The Method

Three focused sessions, each with a clear deliverable.

### Session 1: AI Opportunity Workshop (half day)

We bring together your domain experts, technical team, and decision-makers. The goal is to move from "we want to do AI" to "here is the specific problem we will solve first."

Using a structured scoring framework (value vs. effort vs. data readiness), we identify and prioritize 2-3 concrete use cases from your real operations. Participants leave with a shared definition of the prototype we will build.

**Deliverable:** A prioritized shortlist with a go/no-go decision on one use case.

### Session 2: Technical Design and Build Sprint (full day)

We design the technical architecture for the prototype and start building. This is hands-on - your technical team participates, learns, and contributes. We use AWS services where they fit, open-source where they are better.

By end of day, you have a working skeleton of the prototype.

**Deliverable:** A functional prototype (not a demo, a working prototype with real inputs and real outputs).

### Session 3: Validation and Roadmap (half day)

We test the prototype with real data and real users from your team. We document what works, what needs refinement, and what the path to production looks like. We also assess AWS funding eligibility.

**Deliverable:** A validated prototype, a technical roadmap, and a funding assessment.

## Why 3 Weeks Works

- Short enough to maintain momentum and stakeholder attention
- Long enough to produce something real and testable
- Structured enough to make decisions, not just discuss them
- Flexible enough to adapt to your industry and tech stack

## Who This Is For

Organizations that:
- Have a clear business problem but no clear AI solution
- Have tried AI projects that lost momentum or never shipped
- Need to demonstrate AI value internally before investing in production
- Are interested in AWS-funded PoC development

This is not for organizations looking for a presentation deck. It is for teams ready to build something and show it.
