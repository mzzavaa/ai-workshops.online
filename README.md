## The Three Workshop Method

Most AI projects fail not because the technology doesn't work, but because organizations skip the hard early conversations and jump straight to implementation. The Three Workshop Method structures those conversations into a repeatable process that ends with a working prototype and a team that understands what they built and why.

### Workshop 1: Goal Workshop
Define the problem precisely enough that you know what success looks like. Surface the real process complexity, identify use case candidates, and establish success criteria.

### Workshop 2: Concept Workshop
Technical deep-dive to validate feasibility. Confirm data access, design architecture, identify risks, and scope the prototype.

### Workshop 3: Decision Workshop
Live prototype demonstration with real data. Gather stakeholder feedback, make go/no-go decisions, and define the roadmap.
