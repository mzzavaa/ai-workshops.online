The Method

3 workshops. 3 weeks.
One working prototype.

A proven, structured flow that moves your organization from "we want AI" to a decision-ready prototype - without the months of back-and-forth.

01
Goal Workshop
2 hours

Define the problem, discover the opportunity

We map your organization's AI ambitions to concrete business problems. Using structured facilitation, we identify and prioritize use cases by business value and technical feasibility.

  • Prioritized AI use case list
  • Business value assessment
  • Clear problem statements
02
Concept Workshop
3 hours

Architecture, AWS services, and cost estimates

Technical deep-dive on the top use cases. We design the architecture, select the right AWS AI services, estimate infrastructure costs, and define what a prototype would look like.

  • Architecture concepts
  • AWS service mapping
  • Cost estimates per use case
03
Decision Workshop
2 hours

Live prototype demo and go/no-go decisions

We present a working prototype of the selected use case. Your team evaluates it live, makes go/no-go decisions for each idea, and leaves with a concrete implementation roadmap.

  • Working prototype demo
  • Go/no-go decisions per use case
  • Implementation roadmap
From first call to working prototype in 3 weeks
